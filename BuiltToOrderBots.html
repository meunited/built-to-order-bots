How to Play

  • Pick a monster type - 12 species with unique stats and abilities.
  • Add enhancements - Up to 4: tentacles, sting, fiery breath, wings, etc.
  • Equip a weapon - 11 weapons from sword to plasma rifle.
  • Pick a battlefield - Desert, jungle, or swamp.
  • Pick a mode - Destruction, Flag Capture, or The Horde.
  • Win to earn money. Spend it in tournament mode to build an army.

Controls

  • ← → or A/D : Move
  • ↑ or W or Space : Jump (double-jump with Wings)
  • Click / Tap : Shoot
  • ESC : Pause
